## TLDR: I want to find brain rot with wacky typing game.



I start Typpy because I have a simple idea: 

### I want kids to use their brain more

I had been reading a lot on doom-scrolling, brain rot related articles (I am the victim too). Worry about the future, that we give our kids the kind of platforms, distill their dopemine at the rate of seconds.

Kids these days not only losing their curiosity (which is the most important attribute as human), they are literally losing their common sense. they stick their eyes to the screen at all time, even while walking, not aware of the danger around, make them become the easy crime target. And they also interact less with people within their vicinity.

I am a game designer. I started design party cardgames last year (https://gamedexs.com), targeting to family so that they have **quality play time** with their kids (or even among kids). That is ok, but take some time to build up.

I am also a coder, used to create video games, but video games take too long to produce (logic, animation, UI design, ...).

Then I discovered typing test, which is not a game, yet it has some gaming attribute.

### So the lightbulb moment!

I can create weird, wacky typing games, that everyone can just launch the page and start typing.
I am good at injecting some quirks, to confuse user, thus use their brain (my way of fighting brain rot, hehe.)

### Confession

Initially I want to make my mission sound more **up there**, that typing can improve brain cognitive activities bla bla...

After a little research, turn out **hand writing** is way better than typing in that manners.

a bit of disappointment to found that out. However, I can get everyone to pick up pencil to write, but I hope with this Typpy games, I can at least get people to start typing.

### Thus Typpy.online!

With little help of AI, I created this website. and I will gradually add in more wacky games, taking my small steps.

I hope you enjoy what you see.
